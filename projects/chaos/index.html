Lorenz system

defined by the system of simultaneous differential equations: $$ {\small \begin{cases} \frac{dx}{dt} = \sigma (y - x) \\ \frac{dy}{dt} = x(\rho - z) - y \\ \frac{dz}{dt} = xy - \beta z \end{cases} } $$

Aizawa system

defined by the system of simultaneous differential equations: $$ {\small \begin{cases} \frac{dx}{dt} = (z - b)x - d y \\ \frac{dy}{dt} = d x + (z - b)y \\ \frac{dz}{dt} = c + a z - \frac{z^3}{3} - (x^2 + y^2)(1 + e z) + f z x^3 \end{cases} } $$

Halvorsen system

defined by the system of simultaneous differential equations: $$ {\small \begin{cases} \frac{dx}{dt} = -a x - 4y - 4z - y^2 \\ \frac{dy}{dt} = -a y - 4z - 4x - z^2 \\ \frac{dz}{dt} = -a z - 4x - 4y - x^2 \end{cases} } $$
