About this site

This site contains mathematical problems and investigations arising from such problems. Much of the work is based on using computational experiments to handle heavy calculations, freeing me to observe and explain patterns.

Topics I return to frequently include number theory, real analysis, and fractal geometry. My aim with this site is not to present finished results, but to document patterns, questions, and partial explanations that arise during problem-solving.
